You should make this kind of page for your project. The purpose of the page is to provide important project documentation and links to documents. Here you can explain the goal of your project with a few sentences.

Your teacher will put a link to your project page from the course page. For this reason, you should name your .html file so that its name is, for example, nicegame_project_page.html if the name of your project is called NiceGame. (If you use this page as a basis for your project page, you just replace the word 'sample' with the name of your project.)
